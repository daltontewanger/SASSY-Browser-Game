Run Eat Run!

Welcome to the Strawberry Kingdom!

Objective:

Once upon a berry-luscious time, you reigned as the mighty lord of strawberries, revered by all. But alas, a bitter twist of fate stripped you of your powers! Now, the very strawberries you once ruled see you as a mere mortal and are launching a fruity rebellion against you. To reclaim your title as the almighty lord of strawberries, you must devour them to regain your strength and emerge victorious!

Controls:

Use the arrow keys to navigate your character. Be cautious, though; your speed increases rapidly and slows gradually. Your character seamlessly traverses the screen's edges, so exercise precision to maintain control.

Strawberry Shenanigans:

Strawberries of varying sizes emerge from all sides of the screen. Only munch on those smaller than you to thrive. The closer their size matches yours, the greater your reward. However, avoid strawberries larger than you at all costsâ€”they spell your downfall.

Time to gobble up those strawberries and seize your berry kingdom once more!
